Version: 1.1.0
Date: 15. 07. 2026
  Features:
    - Added native circuit-network connections and an enable condition to the Suspension Coffin.
    - Disabled coffins now immediately eject their occupants and cannot be entered until enabled again.
    - Added a console message explaining when suspension is cancelled by a circuit condition.
  Changes:
    - Added a focused circuit enable-condition panel without lamp-specific controls.
    - Moved the circuit wire connection point toward the lower-right side of the coffin.
  Bugfixes:
    - Fixed occupants not being ejected when a coffin was disabled by its circuit condition.
  Compatibility:
    - Added a migration for Suspension Coffins placed with version 1.0.0.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Version: 1.0.0
Date: 15. 07. 2026
  Major Features:
    - Added the Suspension Coffin, a placeable 4x4 chamber that accelerates the simulation to 100x while occupied.
    - Added a smooth game-speed ramp, animated vignette, and suspension status display.
  Features:
    - Added a dedicated technology unlocked after cryogenic science.
    - Added a 30-second recipe using cold fluoroketone barrels, a cryogenic plant, heating towers, and steel.
    - Prevented suspended players from interacting with inventories or the world while preserving the normal vehicle-exit control.
    - Restricted construction to planetary surfaces.
